## 1. BRAND ESSENCE

### Brand Positioning

Pureleven is:

> A modern organic living brand focused on natural foods, wellness, and everyday healthy living.

The brand should feel:

- **Bright** — Clean, optimistic, approachable
- **Clean** — Simple, uncluttered, modern
- **Natural** — Rooted in organic living
- **Welcoming** — Family-friendly and accessible
- **Family-Friendly** — For everyone in the home
- **Educational** — Ingredient and wellness focused
- **Trustworthy** — Honest sourcing and quality
- **Approachable** — Not intimidating or pretentious

### What Pureleven IS NOT

- ❌ Luxury fashion
- ❌ Dark wellness
- ❌ Ultra-premium editorial
- ❌ Minimal luxury

## 7. THE 70-20-10 COLOR RULE

### How to Use Colors Properly

### 70% — Neutral Backgrounds
- Warm Cream (#F8F5EE)
- White (#FFFFFF)
- Soft Sand (#E8E5DC)

### 20% — Brand Colors
- Primary Teal (#1B5E56)
- Fresh Teal (#4D9B93)

### 10% — Accent Colors
- Terracotta (#B45E3C)
- Honey Gold (#C89A3D)
- Sage (#7FA89F)

This keeps the site:
- Clean
- Bright
- Organic
- Approachable

## 8. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Primary Font: Nunito Sans

**Use For:**
- Headings
- Buttons
- Navigation
- UI labels
- All interface text

**Why It Works:**
- Approachable
- Modern
- Friendly
- Readable
- Clean organic feel

### Secondary Font: Inter

**Use For:**
- Body text
- Product descriptions
- Forms
- Menus
- Educational content
- All long-form copy

**Why It Works:**
- Highly readable
- Clean
- Scalable
- Modern ecommerce friendly
- Excellent accessibility

### Typography Style

The typography should feel:

- Easy to read
- Friendly
- Natural
- Modern
- Not overly premium
- Approachable

### Avoid

- Overly elegant serif fonts
- Fashion-magazine typography
- Dramatic editorial typography

### Typography Scale

| Element | Size | Weight | Usage |
| ------- | ---- | ------ | ----- |
| H1 | 48px | 700 | Page headlines |
| H2 | 36px | 700 | Section headers |
| H3 | 28px | 600 | Subsections |
| H4 | 22px | 600 | Card titles |
| Body | 16px | 400 | Body text |
| Small | 14px | 400 | Labels, captions |
