## Guardrails

- Do not invent first-person farm visits, interviews, quotes, harvest scenes, or customer anecdotes.
- Keep the human touch observational and grounded in verified context.
- If a farming or process claim is not supported by the brief or a cited source, remove it.
- Use external authority sources only when the article writer can actually verify them, with priority on Spices Board India, APEDA, FAO, and ICAR.
- Keep wellness language practical and non-medical.
