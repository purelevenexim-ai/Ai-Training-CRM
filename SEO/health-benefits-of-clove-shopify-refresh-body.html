FAQ

Frequently asked questions about clove

What are the most useful everyday benefits of clove?

In cooking, the big advantages are strong aroma, broad use across dishes, and the fact that a small amount can do a lot of work.

How can I tell if whole cloves are good quality?

Look for intact whole buds and a clear sweet-spicy aroma when the pack is opened.

What dishes are whole cloves best used in?

They fit chai, biryani, pulao, masala blends, stockpots, slow-cooked dishes, and some festive sweets.

How should cloves be stored?

Keep them sealed, dry, and away from heat and steam so the aroma stays stronger for longer.

Which pack size should I buy?

Choose 100gm for moderate household use and 200gm when cloves are already part of your regular cooking rhythm.
