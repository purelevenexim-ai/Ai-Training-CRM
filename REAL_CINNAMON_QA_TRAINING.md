## Top recurring question themes

These are the main questions I see repeated across the raw chats for cinnamon training:

1. Price or rate for cinnamon in 100g, 200g, 250g, 500g, and 1kg packs.
2. Stock availability, especially whether patta / karuvappatta is available now.
3. Combo pricing with cardamom, clove, and black pepper.
4. Delivery and shipping questions, including parcel timing and destination charges.
5. Payment and confirmation follow-ups, including GPay and screenshot requests.
6. Original / Ceylon / quality / grade questions.
7. Wholesale or bulk rate questions.
8. Pack-size questions like 100g, 200g, half kg, and 1kg.

## Cardamom-related questions

## Quick Training Notes
- Customers usually ask for price first.
- Most questions are short and direct, often Malayalam-English mix.
- Stock questions should be answered fast: `theernnirikkuvaanu`, `varumbo parayaatto`.
- Combo offers are very common with cardamom, clove, pepper, and cinnamon.
- Keep replies short and practical, like the real chat style.
