===========================
ABOUT PURELEVEN EXIM
===========================

Q: What is PureLeven Exim?
A: PureLeven Exim is a Kerala-based spice company that sources premium spices directly from farms in Idukki district and ships across India. We sell cardamom, black pepper, Ceylon cinnamon, clove, and turmeric — freshly packed and delivered to your door.

Q: Where are your spices sourced from?
A: All spices are sourced directly from farms in Idukki district, Kerala. Cardamom and pepper come from the Munnar/Wayanad belt. Clove is from Adimali, Idukki. Ceylon cinnamon is sourced directly from Sri Lanka (C5 grade).

Q: Are your spices 100% natural and chemical-free?
A: Yes. No artificial colour, no fillers, no additives. All products are farm-sourced, washed, cleaned, and graded before packing.

Q: Is your cardamom A+ grade and original?
A: Yes. Our cardamom is 8.5mm A+ Export Grade sourced directly from Idukki farms. No artificial colour, no additives, 100% natural. Approximately 70% full pods per pack.

Q: Is your cinnamon real Ceylon cinnamon or cassia?
A: It is original Ceylon Cinnamon (C5 grade) — Cinnamomum verum, not cassia. Sourced directly from Sri Lanka. Much healthier and more fragrant than common cassia cinnamon.

Q: Is your black pepper from Idukki?
A: Yes. First-quality Idukki black pepper — washed, cleaned, and graded. No stones, no dust. Pure Idukki produce.

Q: Is your turmeric powder pure?
A: Yes. Pure turmeric powder with no fillers. Farm-sourced natural colour from our own turmeric farm and production.

Q: Is your clove high-quality?
A: Yes. Our clove is from Adimali, Idukki, known for high essential oil content. Fresh, aromatic, no stems, no fillers, no adulteration.
