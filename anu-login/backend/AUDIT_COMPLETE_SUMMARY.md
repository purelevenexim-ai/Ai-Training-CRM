## 🎓 LESSONS FOR FUTURE

### How to Prevent Hallucinations
1. **Provide real data as input** (website facts, not imagination)
2. **Have AI cite sources** (quote where facts come from)
3. **Require human verification** (especially for pricing, contact, testimonials)
4. **Use primary sources** (official website > marketing copy > word-of-mouth)
5. **Implement fact-checking** (before deploying to customers)

### What's High-Risk
- ❌ Pricing (always fact-check against website)
- ❌ Contact info (verify it works)
- ❌ Customer testimonials (get real reviews)
- ❌ Guarantees (match actual policy)
- ❌ Competitor claims (avoid or verify)
- ❌ Certifications (state exactly what you have)

### What's Safe-ish
- ✓ Brand voice/tone (based on real communication)
- ✓ Story/narrative (if referencing real events)
