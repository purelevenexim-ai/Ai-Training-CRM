## SIDE-BY-SIDE COMPARISON

### **Traffic Source Differences**

| Aspect | Google Ads Visitors | Meta Ads Visitors |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **User Intent** | HIGH (actively searching) | MEDIUM (interest-based) |
| **Purchase Intent** | 20-40% in first visit | 5-10% in first visit |
| **Awareness Level** | Know your brand exists | May not know brand yet |
| **Decision Stage** | Evaluation/Purchase phase | Awareness/Consideration phase |
| **Purchase Urgency** | Higher (now buying) | Lower (exploring options) |
| **Price Sensitivity** | Less (already decided) | More (still comparing) |

### **Audience Psychology**

```
GOOGLE ADS VISITOR:
├─ Searched for problem → Found your ad → Clicked
├─ Already knows they need solution
├─ Considering options (including competitors)
├─ Skepticism: "Is this the best solution?"
├─ Main objection: Price, delivery time, trust
└─ Mindset: "Help me buy the right product"

META ADS VISITOR:
├─ Saw interesting post → Got curious → Clicked
├─ May not have felt urgent need before
├─ Just discovered your brand today
├─ Skepticism: "Do I even need this?"
├─ Main objection: Need, quality, value, risk
└─ Mindset: "Why should I care about this?"
```

### **Retargeting Message Strategy**

```
GOOGLE ADS RETARGETING MESSAGING:
├─ Ad 1: Urgency ("Complete your order now")
├─ Ad 2: Value ("Look at your savings")
├─ Ad 3: Reassurance ("See our guarantees")
├─ Ad 4: Social proof ("1,000+ happy customers")
└─ Tone: Direct, action-oriented, time-sensitive

META ADS RETARGETING MESSAGING:
├─ Ad 1: Education ("Why real cinnamon matters")
├─ Ad 2: Social proof ("What others discovered")
├─ Ad 3: Lifestyle ("Make your mornings better")
├─ Ad 4: Benefits ("Health benefits of cinnamon")
├─ Ad 5: Offer ("Try our bundle deal")
└─ Tone: Inspirational, educational, lifestyle-focused
```

## TRACKING SETUP: Making It Work Together

### **JavaScript Captures Both Sources**

```
When someone visits pureleven.com:

JavaScript detects:
├─ utm_source = "google_ads" 
│  → Tracked as Google Ads visitor
│  → Added to Google Ads audiences
│  → Retargeted with Google system
│
├─ utm_source = "facebook"
│  → Tracked as Meta ads visitor  
│  → Added to Meta ads audiences
│  → Retargeted with Meta system
│
└─ utm_source = "[other]"
    → General visitor audience
    → Standard retargeting
```

```

Daily you export CSVs:
├─ CSV1: All google_ads visitors → Google Ads retargeting
```
