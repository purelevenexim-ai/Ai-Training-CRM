## 🎓 KEY LEARNING

**The Problem with AI Content:**
- AI can sound confident about things it doesn't know
- Sounds like real data even when hallucinated
- Hard to catch without verification against sources
- Pricing, contact info, testimonials are HIGH RISK

**The Solution:**
1. Always provide real data as input to AI
2. Have AI reference sources explicitly
3. Require human verification (especially for):
   - Contact information
   - Pricing
   - Customer testimonials
   - Guarantees/promises
   - Competitor claims
4. Use website scraping/APIs to keep data fresh
5. Have legal review for any claims made
