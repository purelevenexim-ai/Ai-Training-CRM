## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE SUMMARY

```
MONTHLY BUDGET ALLOCATION (Example: ₹5000/month):

₹1500 - Checkout Abandoners (30% of budget)
        └─ Highest ROI, most urgent

₹1000 - Cart Abandoners (20% of budget)
        └─ High ROI, second priority

₹1500 - Non-Converters (30% of budget)
        └─ Building awareness, steady ROI

₹800 - Product-Specific (16% of budget)
       └─ Targeted engagement

₹200 - Testing/New Audiences (4% of budget)
       └─ Experiments for scaling
```

## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **Start Small**: Launch with checkout abandoners first (highest ROI)
2. **Track Everything**: Use UTM codes to measure success
3. **Iterate**: Pause low performers, double down on winners
4. **Refresh Audiences**: Update daily (checkout/cart), weekly (product viewers)
5. **Creative Fatigue**: Change visuals every 2 weeks to avoid ad blindness
6. **Frequency Cap**: Don't overwhelm people with ads
7. **Budget Flexibility**: Be ready to shift budget based on performance
