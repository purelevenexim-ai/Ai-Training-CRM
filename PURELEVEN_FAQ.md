## 🌿 About PureLeven Exim

**Q: Who is PureLeven Exim?**
A: PureLeven Exim is a Kerala-based spice company that sources premium spices directly from farms in Idukki district — one of India's most prized spice-growing regions. We sell cardamom, black pepper, Ceylon cinnamon, clove, and turmeric, freshly packed and delivered across India.

**Q: Where are your spices sourced from?**
A: All our spices are sourced directly from farms in Idukki district, Kerala. Our cardamom and pepper come from the Munnar/Wayanad belt. Our clove is from Adimali, Idukki. Our Ceylon cinnamon is sourced directly from Sri Lanka (C5 grade).

**Q: Are your products 100% natural?**
A: Yes. No artificial colour, no fillers, no additives. All products are farm-sourced, washed, cleaned, and graded before packing.
