## Upgrade goals

1. Give combo-pack shoppers a direct comparison article instead of forcing product-page hopping.
2. Reduce hesitation between the starter bundle and the larger refill bundle.
3. Support gifting, first-order, and refill-intent traffic.
4. Tie the combo line into the existing storage and spice-quality articles.

## Section structure

1. Quick answer band
- Define who each combo fits.

2. Human hook
- Frame the bundle choice as a kitchen setup decision, not a discount decision.

3. Comparison table
- Starter versus larger pack by use case, quantity, and buyer fit.

4. Use-case blocks
- Gifting, first kitchen setup, regular refill.

5. Storage and follow-up guidance
- Explain what to do after opening the bundle.

6. FAQ block
- Focus on bundle logic and storage.

7. Soft CTA
- Route to both combo packs and the combo collection.
