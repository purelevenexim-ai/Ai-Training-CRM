**Step 1: Navigate to Blog**
1. Go to https://rwxtic-gz.myshopify.com/admin
2. Click **Content** → **Blog posts**
3. Click **Cooking & Spices** blog
4. Click **Add blog post**

**Step 2: Copy Article Content**
- **Blog post title:** Kerala Spices: Farm-Origin Quality vs Generic Online Retailers
- **Blog post content:** Copy entire HTML from `/tmp/blog_article.json` (bodyHtml field)
- **Blog post excerpt:** Discover why farm-origin Kerala spices from Pureleven outperform retail-focused platforms...

**Step 3: Add Featured Image**
- Click **Add image**
- Upload your image (recommended: 1200x630px for social sharing)
- Alt text: "Farm-origin Kerala spices vs generic retail spices comparison"

**Step 4: Add Metadata**
- **SEO title:** Kerala Spices: Farm-Origin Quality vs Generic Online Retailers
- **SEO description:** Discover why farm-origin Kerala spices from Pureleven outperform retail-focused platforms. Compare cultivation, freshness, aroma quality, and sourcing practices.

**Step 5: Add Tags**

- Then click **Publish** when ready for live
