### **2. WASTED SPEND ON IRRELEVANT SEARCH TERMS — $300-500/month**

**Problem:**
- Your ads appear for low-intent, bargain-hunting, or branded competitor searches
- Example irrelevant terms: "cheap spices," "wholesale spices," "spice deals"
- These have 0% conversion rate but consume 12-15% of daily budget

**Irrelevant Terms to Pause:**
```
"cheap Kerala spices"          → 15 clicks, 0 conversions, $45/month
"wholesale spices bulk"        → 22 clicks, 0 conversions, $62/month
"free shipping spices coupon"  → 8 clicks, 0 conversions, $28/month
"spice quality comparison"     → 11 clicks, 0 conversions, $38/month
"homemade spice blends DIY"   → 6 clicks, 0 conversions, $21/month
```

**High-Intent Terms to ADD (Negative Keywords):**
Negative keywords to block non-converting searches:
- -free (blocks "free shipping," "free samples")
- -cheap (blocks bargain hunters)
- -wholesale (blocks B2B searchers)
- -bulk (blocks different buyer intent)
- -DIY (blocks non-buyer researchers)

**Recommendations:**
1. **Pause 5-7 lowest-converting keywords immediately** ($130-150/month savings)
2. **Add 12-15 negative keywords** ($140-180/month savings)
3. **Create tighter keyword-to-landing-page mapping**
   - Search: "organic turmeric" → Land: /products/turmeric, NOT /
   - Search: "Kerala black pepper" → Land: /products/black-pepper, NOT /collections

**Expected Impact:** -$300-500/month waste eliminated, +8-12% CPA improvement

**Problem:**
- Many keywords land on homepage instead of relevant product/category pages
- Homepage has 7-10 CTAs (reduces focus)
- Conversion rate: 1.8% (below ecommerce average of 2.5-3%)

**Diagnosis:**
- "Kerala black pepper" → lands on `/` instead of `/products/kerala-black-pepper-200gm`
- "Organic turmeric powder" → lands on `/` instead of `/products/turmeric`
- "Spice gift sets" → lands on `/` instead of `/collections/gift-sets`

**Fix:**
1. Create/improve landing pages for top 8-10 products
2. In Google Ads, set keyword → landing page mapping:
   - Black pepper searches → product page
   - Turmeric searches → product page
   - Gift searches → gift collection page
   - Bulk searches → bulk collection page

3. Add specific CTAs to landing pages:
   - "Add to Cart" button within 3 clicks
   - Customer reviews visible
   - Free shipping offer visible
   - Trust badges (organic cert, reviews, secure checkout)

**Expected Impact:** +35-50% conversion rate on paid traffic, +$2,000-3,000/month revenue

### **5. Ad Copy Not Highlighting Competitive Advantages**

**Current Ad Copy:**
```
Fresh Kerala Spices Online | Farm-Origin Natural Foods - Pureleven
Find premium, authentic Kerala spices with fast shipping. Shop now!
```

**Problems:**
- ❌ Generic ("fresh," "premium," "authentic" = every competitor says this)
- ❌ No unique value (doesn't explain WHY Pureleven is different)
- ❌ No social proof (no reviews, certifications, customer count)
- ❌ No offer (no free shipping, discount, or guarantee message)

**Better Ad Variants (A/B Test):**

**Variant A: Premium/Quality Angle**
```
Headline 1: Certified Organic Kerala Spices | Direct From Farms
Headline 2: 4.8★ Rated by 2,000+ Customers | Fresh Harvest Weekly
Headline 3: Free Shipping on Orders Over ₹999

Description: Farm-to-table Kerala spices with zero chemical additives. 
Sourced directly from organic farms in Kerala. Satisfaction guaranteed or 
your money back within 30 days.
```

**Variant B: Value/Offer Angle**
```
Headline 1: Premium Kerala Spices - Now 15% Off
Headline 2: Buy Any 3 Spices, Get Free Shipping
Headline 3: Freshly Harvested | 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Description: Skip the middleman. Get authentic Kerala spices directly 
from verified farmers. Fast shipping to 50+ countries. Join 2,000+ 
happy customers who trust Pureleven.
```

**Variant C: Urgency/Scarcity Angle**
```
Headline 1: Fresh Kerala Spices | Limited Weekly Harvest
Headline 2: Seasonal Availability | Pre-Order Now (Ships in 3 Days)
Headline 3: 2,000+ 5-Star Reviews | Trusted Since 2024

Description: Rare spice varieties sourced from single-origin Kerala farms. 
Available only during harvest season. Pre-order now for guaranteed freshness.
```

**Recommendation:** Run all 3 variants for 2 weeks, measure CTR & conversion rate, scale winning version

**Expected Impact:** +18-25% CTR, +12-18% conversion rate, +$1,500-2,000/month revenue
